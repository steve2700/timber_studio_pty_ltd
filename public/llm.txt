## Money-Saving Tips to Share

1. **Keep existing layout** - Save R15,000-R50,000 by not moving plumbing/electrical
2. **Reface cabinets** - Save 40-50% vs full replacement if boxes are sound
3. **Mix high & low** - Splurge on visible items, save on hidden elements
4. **Time purchases** - Black Friday, year-end sales (save 10-20%)
5. **DIY demolition** - Save R10,000-R30,000 on labor
6. **Get multiple quotes** - Compare 3-5 quotes (save 15-30%)
7. **Choose local granite** - Save 30-40% vs imported stone
8. **20mm thickness** - More affordable than 30mm, still durable
